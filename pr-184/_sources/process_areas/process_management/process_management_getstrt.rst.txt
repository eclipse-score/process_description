Getting Started
###############

.. doc_getstrt:: Getting Started on Process Management
   :id: doc_getstrt__process_management_process
   :status: valid
   :tags: process_management

This document describes the steps to create and maintain the process strategy and the
process description.

Therefore guidelines :need:`gd_guidl__process_management` and
a concept :need:`doc_concept__process_management_process` are available.

General Workflow
****************

The workflows are defined in the :ref:`process_management_workflows` section.

For every change identified, the following workflows are executed:

* Create and maintain process management strategy according to :need:`wf__cr_mt_proc_mgt_strategy`
* Define and approves your process definition according to :need:`wf__def_app_process_definition`
* Implement change request and monitor it until closure according to :need:`wf__change_implement_monitor_cr`
* Close the change request according to :need:`wf__change_close_cr`

In addition create a change management plan as part of the platform management plan according to :need:`wf__platform_cr_mt_platform_mgmt_plan`
